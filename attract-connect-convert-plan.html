🐢
The Steady Method: Attraction does the heavy lifting. DMs build relationships — never lead with selling. Earn trust first. Offers only surface naturally, or through a free entry point. No cold pitching, ever.
Phase 1
Attract
Content brings the right people to you. They raise their hand by engaging, following, or joining your community.
Phase 2
Connect
DMs and community deepen the relationship. Give value, show up consistently, and build genuine trust over time.
Phase 3
Convert
When it feels natural — or they ask — a free offer or working together comes up. They convert because they already trust you.
Your philosophy in practice
🧲
Let content do the outreach
You don't go looking for clients — you create content so consistently and valuably that clients come looking for you.
💬
DMs are conversations, not funnels
When someone engages, respond like a human. Ask questions, be curious, add value. No script, no agenda.
🤝
Trust before offers
The offer comes after the relationship, not before. Only when the conversation naturally arrives there.
🎁
Free offer as a bridge, not a hook
A free resource serves the person first. If they love it, the paid path becomes obvious — you don't need to point to it.
